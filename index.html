Client Guidelines

We’re so glad you’re here! Back on the Rack is a hassle-free way to on-sell your quality preloved clothing — no product photography, no chasing messages, just a lovely retail space doing the work for you.

📅 How It Works

  • Bring up to 20 items — we’ll display 15 at a time and top up as pieces sell.
  • Your items stay on display for a 6-week cycle.
  • Delivery by arrangement with Amy — get in touch to lock in your drop-off time.

💳 Fees & Payments

  • $20 rack fee per cycle, payable before drop-off (cash or direct deposit).
  • 15% commission applies to each item sold.
  • Payment made to you within 4 weeks of your cycle end.
  • Price items to already include the 15% — that’s the tag price customers see.

📷 Garment Standards

  • All clothing must be clean, freshly ironed and in full working order.
  • Items with marks, stains, holes, rips, missing buttons or faulty zips will be set aside for collection.

🏷 Presentation

  • We supply hangers and attach swing tags with your sale details.
  • Bring a completed item list at drop-off: brand, size, price (inc. commission) and a short description.
  • Use our online form below or the hard copy form provided at drop-off.

📦 After Your Cycle

  • We’ll text you when your cycle wraps up.
  • Please collect unsold items within 2 weeks.
  • Anything remaining after 2 weeks will be donated to the Op Shop.

📏 Sizing & Categories

  • We aim to keep an inclusive size range — your spot timing may vary based on current stock mix.
  • Let us know your size range when joining the waitlist.
  • Currently ladies’ clothing only. Vintage, kids & more coming soon!
